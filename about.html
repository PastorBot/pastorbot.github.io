About Pastor Bot

Background

Successfully detecting trend(s) in data can both reveal unfortunate truths and lead to significant opportunities.

For example, the decline of religion in the U.S., expecially Christianity, is well documented. Because churches play an outsized roll in humanitarian work, healthy community buidling, and moral education, well-attended and prosperous churches tend to benefit both society as a whole and people individually. Thus, counteracting the degredation of Christianity's popularity is of great importance.

Fortunately, a more recent yet concurrent trend may offer a solution to counteract the decline in American churches and bolster American Christian convictions: the rise in generative artificial intelligence or "GenAI". Technological breakthroughs have always reshaped societies, and GenAI's exceptional ability to collect and communicate relevant information in a superior way to previous information aggregators / search engines has opened and will continue to open new doors.

As general feelings of loneliness and a lack of meaning proliferate in American society, people are begining to overcome religious apathy and seek faith. Pastor Bot is a tool for such people, providing an on-ramp to organized Christianity by answering personalized life questions using scriptural insights.

Technology

Pastor Bot leverages Meta's Llama 3 large language model, with the Bible and hundreds of pastor-realistic Q&A pairs as its knowledge base.

Next Steps

Pastor Bot will continue to be refined and improved, extended to other platforms, and applied to new use cases.
